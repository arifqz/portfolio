About

Arif makes 3D illustrations using Blender3D, drawing on his experience as an undocumented immigrant. Based in New York City with his cat, he integrates nostalgia and innovation in his work. A 2023 New York Foundation for the Arts fellow, Arif has collaborated with the Poetry Foundation, MTA, The New York Times, The New Yorker, Business Insider, School of Visual Arts, and CashApp..
